# **App Name**: Riyogya

## Core Features:

- Hero Section Display: Showcase the main value proposition, title 'Riyogya – Smart SaaS Solutions for Modern Businesses', a short description about AI automation, ERP systems, and WhatsApp communication tools, with a 'Get Started' call-to-action button.
- Informational Content Sections: Display the 'About', 'Solutions', and 'Features' sections. The 'Solutions' section will use a card layout for 'Hospital Management System', 'School ERP System', 'Business Automation Tools', and 'WhatsApp Communication Platform'. The 'Features' section will highlight 'Cloud-based platform', 'Secure data management', 'Automation workflows', 'WhatsApp Business API integration', and 'Real-time analytics'.
- Contact and Footer Sections: Present the 'Contact' section with Business name (Riyogya), location (Sindhanur, Karnataka, India), email placeholder, and phone placeholder. The 'Footer' includes '© Riyogya' copyright text, 'Privacy Policy', and 'Terms of Service' links.
- Fully Responsive Design: Implement a fully responsive design to ensure the website adapts seamlessly and displays optimally across various devices, including mobile and desktop.
- AI Assistant Tool for FAQ: Integrate a simple AI-powered chatbot tool, accessible via the contact section, that provides instant answers to common questions about Riyogya's services by leveraging a pre-trained language model to process inquiries and offer concise, relevant information.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary action color: A professional and tech-inspired blue (#4D7FDB), conveying trust and innovation.
- Background color: A very light, subtle blue-tinted white (#F3F6FC), ensuring a clean, modern, and spacious aesthetic reminiscent of leading SaaS platforms.
- Accent color: A rich, complementary purple (#663399) to provide visual contrast and highlight important elements like call-to-action buttons or section headers.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for a modern, tech-forward, and bold appearance for titles and major headings.
- Body text font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for exceptional readability and a clean, neutral, and professional feel for all body content and descriptions.
- Utilize simple, geometric line icons that align with a minimalist and contemporary SaaS visual style, ensuring clarity and unobtrusiveness.
- Implement a clean, grid-based layout with generous white space to enhance readability and achieve a modern SaaS aesthetic, similar to Stripe or Notion. Ensure elements are well-aligned with a clear visual hierarchy.
- Incorporate subtle hover animations on interactive elements such as buttons, navigation links, and solution cards to provide gentle user feedback and add a refined, dynamic touch without distracting from the content.
